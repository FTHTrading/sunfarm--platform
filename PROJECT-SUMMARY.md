# PROJECT SUMMARY — SunFarm PV 50 MW

**One-Page Investor Overview**

---

## The Opportunity

**SunFarm PV** is a 50 MW AC (59.69 MWp DC) utility-scale solar photovoltaic project with integrated 25 MW / 100 MWh battery energy storage, located on 440 hectares of titled agricultural land in Baní, Peravia Province, Dominican Republic.

The project holds a **definitive 25-year concession** (CNE-CP-0012-2020) from the Comisión Nacional de Energía, with all primary regulatory approvals in place.

## Why This Project

1. **Definitive Concession** — Not provisional. 25-year term with construction and operation rights.
2. **Titled Land** — Certificación de No Superposición confirms clean title on 440 ha.
3. **Grid Access Secured** — ETED No-Objection for 138kV interconnection.
4. **Revenue Diversification** — 4+ revenue streams reduce PPA dependency.
5. **BESS Compliance** — 25 MW / 100 MWh meets Decreto 517-25 mandate.
6. **Tax Shield** — 10-year income tax exemption under Ley 57-07.
7. **Proven Technology** — Bifacial 760W modules, LFP batteries, standard inverters.
8. **Carbon Upside** — 73,100 tCO₂/yr eligible for Verra VCS or Gold Standard.

## Entity Structure

```
HoldCo (Master Holding)
├── LandCo — Holds 440 ha titled land. Leases to Energy SPV.
├── Energy SPV — Holds concession, PPA, BESS, carbon rights.
└── Token SPV — Issues revenue-share tokens (Reg D 506(c)).
```

Land title is structurally isolated from token holders. Token SPV receives revenue participation only.

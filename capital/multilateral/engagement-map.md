## Institution Profiles

### IFC (International Finance Corporation)

- **Parent**: World Bank Group
- **Mandate**: Private sector development in emerging markets
- **Relevance**: Caribbean renewable energy program, A/B loan structure enables commercial bank mobilization
- **Entry Requirements**: Environmental & Social Performance Standards (IFC PS 1-8), ESAP, E&S due diligence
- **Instrument**: A-loan (IFC own account) + B-loan (mobilized commercial)
- **Value-Add**: Crowd-in effect, political comfort, PS compliance = bankability signal

### IDB Invest

- **Parent**: Inter-American Development Bank Group
- **Mandate**: Private sector infrastructure in Latin America and Caribbean
- **Relevance**: Dominican Republic country program, NDC alignment
- **Entry Requirements**: Environmental and Social Sustainability Policy, gender lens
- **Instrument**: Senior debt, subordinated debt, equity, guarantees
- **Value-Add**: Local market knowledge, policy dialogue with DR government

### U.S. DFC (Development Finance Corporation)

- **Mandate**: U.S. foreign development investment
- **Relevance**: Caribbean energy security, climate finance mandate
- **Entry Requirements**: U.S. nexus (equipment procurement or parent entity); ESMS
- **Instrument**: Direct loans, loan guarantees, political risk insurance, equity
- **Value-Add**: Political risk insurance at sovereign level, DFC + MIGA stacking possible

### MIGA (Multilateral Investment Guarantee Agency)

- **Parent**: World Bank Group
- **Mandate**: Political risk insurance for cross-border investments
- **Coverage**: Expropriation, transfer restriction, breach of contract, war & civil disturbance
- **Relevance**: DR sovereign risk mitigation, lender comfort
- **Instrument**: Non-honoring of sovereign financial obligation, breach of contract guarantee
- **Value-Add**: 50-100 bps spread reduction on commercial debt

### Green Climate Fund (GCF)

- **Mandate**: Climate mitigation and adaptation in developing countries
- **Relevance**: Caribbean SIDS climate vulnerability, NDC support
- **Access**: Through accredited entities (IDB, IFC, CAF, national entities)
- **Instrument**: Concessional loans (below-market rate), grants, guarantees
- **Value-Add**: Blended finance layer, dramatically reduces WACC

### Climate Investment Funds (CIF)

- **Mandate**: Climate transformation in developing countries
- **Programs**: Clean Technology Fund (CTF), Scaling Up Renewable Energy (SREP)
- **Relevance**: Caribbean CTF allocation, solar + storage eligibility
- **Instrument**: Concessional debt (1-2% interest, 20-year tenor)
- **Value-Add**: Patient capital, extended grace periods

# Green Bond — ICMA GBP Compliance Checklist

> **Classification: CONFIDENTIAL — Bond Structuring**

## ICMA Green Bond Principles — June 2025

**Source:** [ICMA Green Bond Principles (June 2025)](https://www.icmagroup.org/assets/documents/Sustainable-finance/2025-updates/Green-Bond-Principles-GBP-June-2025.pdf)

---

## Pillar 1: Use of Proceeds

| Requirement | SunFarm Status | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Proceeds used for eligible green projects | ✅ | Solar PV + BESS — renewable energy generation and storage |
| Clear environmental benefit | ✅ | ~63,000 tCO₂/yr avoided; displaces fossil fuel grid electricity |
| Eligible categories identified | ✅ | Renewable energy, energy efficiency (BESS), clean transportation (grid services) |
| TVRD alignment | ✅ | Solar PV and BESS classified under Taxonomía Verde RD |
| EU Taxonomy alignment | ✅ | Solar PV substantial contribution to climate mitigation |

**See:** [capital/green-bond/eligible-use-of-proceeds.md](../green-bond/eligible-use-of-proceeds.md) for $55M CAPEX allocation detail

## Pillar 2: Process for Project Evaluation and Selection

| Requirement | SunFarm Status | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Environmental objectives communicated | ✅ | Climate change mitigation, renewable energy transition |
| Process for determining eligibility | ✅ | TVRD alignment assessment completed |
| Exclusion criteria defined | ✅ | No fossil fuel, nuclear, large hydro, or controversial activities |
| Environmental & social risk management | ✅ | EIA approved (License 0379-20), no displacement, no protected areas |

---

## Pillar 3: Management of Proceeds

| Requirement | SunFarm Status | Action Needed |
|---|---|---|
| Net proceeds tracked to eligible projects | ⬜ Pending | Establish dedicated proceeds account with fiduciary oversight |
| Sub-accounts or portfolio approach | ⬜ Pending | Recommend dedicated sub-account per use category |
| Temporary placement policy | ⬜ Pending | Define policy for unallocated proceeds (money market / treasury) |
| Periodic adjustment and reallocation | ⬜ Pending | Annual review of allocation vs. disbursement |

---

## Pillar 4: Reporting

| Requirement | SunFarm Status | Action Needed |
|---|---|---|
| Annual allocation reporting | ⬜ Pending | Template needed — proceeds allocated by category |
| Impact reporting | ⬜ Pending | Template needed — MWh, tCO₂, jobs, community benefit |
| Qualitative + quantitative metrics | ⬜ Pending | Define KPI framework |
| External audit of allocation | ⬜ Pending | Engage external auditor or independent verifier |

## Cross-Framework Compliance

| Framework | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|

## Pre-Issuance Checklist

- [ ] Complete TVRD alignment assessment ✅ (`docs/regulatory/Taxonomia-Verde-RD-Alignment.md`)
- [ ] Document eligible use of proceeds ✅ (`capital/green-bond/eligible-use-of-proceeds.md`)
- [ ] Prepare Green Bond Framework document (for SPO engagement)
- [ ] Engage SPO provider
- [ ] Establish dedicated proceeds account
- [ ] Define temporary placement policy for unallocated proceeds
- [ ] Prepare impact reporting template (annual)
- [ ] Engage external verifier / auditor for allocation reporting
- [ ] Confirm legal opinion on green bond enforceability
- [ ] Prepare investor roadshow materials

---

*This checklist aligns with ICMA Green Bond Principles June 2025 edition. The project satisfies Pillars 1-2 on substance; Pillars 3-4 require implementation of tracking and reporting infrastructure.*

```
│

│   ├── BOS installation (monthly)
│   └── Commissioning holdback (5%)
│
├── BESS Procurement
│   ├── Manufacturing deposit (20%)
│   ├── Delivery payment (60%)
│   └── Installation & commissioning (20%)
│
├── Transmission & Grid
│   ├── ETED interconnection costs
│   ├── Substation equipment
│   └── Line construction
│
└── Debt Service Reserve Account
    └── 6-month reserve (funded at close)
```

---

## Exclusions

The following expenditures are explicitly excluded from Green Bond proceeds:

- Land acquisition costs (funded by LandCo / sponsor equity)
- Token issuance costs (funded by Token SPV)
- Working capital or general corporate purposes
- Dividends or distributions to equity holders
- Refinancing of existing indebtedness
- Fossil fuel-related equipment or infrastructure

---

## Look-Back Period

Eligible expenditures incurred up to 24 months prior to issuance date may be included in the allocation of proceeds, provided they meet all eligibility criteria and have not been previously financed by another green or labeled instrument.

---

## Verification

- **Pre-issuance**: SPO provider confirms framework alignment and proceeds eligibility
- **Post-issuance**: Annual independent audit of proceeds allocation
- **Reporting**: Annual allocation report and impact report published to bondholders

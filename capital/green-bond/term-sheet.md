## Conditions Precedent to Issuance

1. Executed PPA with investment-grade or government-backed off-taker
2. Fixed-price EPC contract with performance guarantees
3. Environmental license current (License 0379-20)
4. ETED grid interconnection agreement
5. Second Party Opinion on green bond framework
6. MIGA or DFC guarantee commitment (if targeting investment grade)
7. Independent engineer report (Mott MacDonald, DNV, or equivalent)
8. Legal opinion on enforceability (NY law counsel)
9. Tax opinion on Ley 57-07 incentive applicability

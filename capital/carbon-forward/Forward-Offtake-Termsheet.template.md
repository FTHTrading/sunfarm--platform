## Risk Allocation

| Risk | Allocation | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Generation underperformance** | Seller | Conservative capacity factor (24%); BESS augmentation |
| **Grid emission factor change** | Shared | Floor price protects seller; ceiling protects buyer |
| **Registry risk** | Seller | Verra is industry standard; methodology is well-established |
| **Delivery shortfall** | Seller (best-efforts) | 80% minimum commitment provides flexibility |
| **Price risk** | Shared (collar) | Floor/ceiling structure eliminates tail risk |
| **Counterparty credit** | Buyer | Require investment-grade buyer or escrow |

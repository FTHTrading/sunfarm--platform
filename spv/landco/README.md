### Purpose

LandCo holds all real property assets. It is the sole titled owner of Parcela 59 DC 08, Baní. No energy concession, PPA, or token exposure exists at this entity. Clean separation of land risk from energy and securities risk.

### Revenue Streams

1. **Highway Frontage Sales** — Sell 40% of frontage (107,070 m²) at ~$90/m² → ~$10M immediate liquidity
2. **Agricultural Leasing** — 200 ha at $400/ha/yr → $80K/yr base, escalating 3%
3. **Energy SPV Lease** — 161 ha leased to Energy SPV at $1,200/ha/yr → $193K/yr base
4. **Expansion Phase** — 80 ha reserved for Phase 2 solar, valued at Year 8 after proven asset appreciation

### Structural Rules

- LandCo **never** transfers title to Energy SPV — lease only
- LandCo **never** has token exposure
- LandCo **never** guarantees Energy SPV debt
- LandCo retains expansion rights and all non-concession land
- LandCo can monetize frontage independently without Energy SPV consent

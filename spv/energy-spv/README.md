# SPV Structure — Energy SPV

## SunFarm Energy, S.R.L.

### Purpose

Energy SPV holds all energy-generation assets: the 50 MW definitive concession, power purchase agreement, BESS, grid interconnection rights, and carbon credit entitlements. It does NOT hold land title (leased from LandCo). It does NOT issue tokens (Token SPV only).

### Assets & Contracts

| Asset | Detail |
|---|---|
| Concession | CNE-CP-0012-2020 — Definitive, 25 years |
| Capacity | 50 MW AC / 59.69 MWp DC |
| Technology | 78,500 × 760W Bifacial modules |
| BESS | 25 MW / 100 MWh LFP (4-hour duration) |
| Grid POI | 138kV bar — Peravia Solar I & II |
| Transmission | 4.5 km double-circuit 138kV line |
| ETED No-Objection | Feb 2024, revalidated May 2025 |
| Environmental License | 0379-20 |
| Carbon Rights | All avoided emissions from generation |

### Capital Structure

| Source | Amount | % |
|---|---|---|
| Senior Secured Debt | $33.0M | 60% |

| **Total CAPEX** | **$55.0M** | **100%** |

### Debt Terms

- Tenor: 18 years
- Rate: 6.5% fixed
- Annual Debt Service: ~$3.16M
- Min DSCR Covenant: 1.25x
- Security: Assignment of PPA, BESS, revenue accounts

### Lease from LandCo

- 161 hectares for solar array
- $1,200/ha/yr escalating 2%
- 25-year term (matching concession)
- No title transfer — Energy SPV is tenant only

### Regulatory Obligations

- Construction start: within 6 months of contract signing
- Construction complete: within 24 months of start
- BESS mandatory: all projects >20 MW (since May 2023)
- Environmental compliance: License 0379-20
- Annual CNE reporting

### Key Laws

- Ley General de Electricidad 125-01
- Ley 186-07 (market regulation)
- Ley 57-07 (renewable energy incentives — 10 yr tax exemption)
- Decreto 202-08, 717-08, 517-25

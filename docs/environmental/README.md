- **Conditions**: Annual environmental monitoring and reporting

- **Ecological Sensitivity**: Low
- **Protected Areas**: None identified within project boundary

1. **Agrivoltaic Design** — Agricultural co-use between and under panels
2. **Vegetation Management** — Grazing and native ground cover
3. **Stormwater** — Drainage plan per license conditions
4. **Waste Management** — Module recycling plan at decommissioning
5. **Community Benefit** — Local employment, agricultural partnerships

## Carbon Credit Program

### Estimated Annual Reduction
- **~73,100 tCO₂e/year** (grid emission factor × generation)

### Certification Options
| Standard | Base Price | Escalation | 25-Year Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Verra VCS | $12/tCO₂ | 3%/yr | ~$25M |
| Gold Standard | $20/tCO₂ | 4%/yr | ~$52M |
| Compliance | $30/tCO₂ | 5%/yr | ~$92M |
| Voluntary Premium | $45/tCO₂ | 6%/yr | ~$163M |

### Registration Steps
1. Develop Project Design Document (PDD)
2. Validation by accredited auditor
3. Registration with Verra VCS or Gold Standard
4. Annual monitoring and verification
5. Credit issuance and retirement/sale

## 8. CNE Concession Transfer Process

Under Ley 125-01 and applicable CNE regulations:

1. **Application**: Joint application by Buyer and Seller to CNE
2. **Technical Review**: CNE evaluates Buyer's technical and financial qualifications
3. **Public Notice**: 30-day public comment period (Gaceta Oficial)
4. **Resolution**: CNE issues resolution approving or denying transfer
5. **Registration**: New concession holder registered with CNE
6. **Timeline**: Estimated 90-120 days from application to resolution

### Transfer Risks

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| CNE denial | Low | Pre-consultation with CNE; Buyer meets all requirements |
| Extended timeline | Medium | Engage specialized DR energy counsel |
| Conditions imposed | Medium | Budget for additional compliance costs |
| Partial transfer | Low | SPA contingent on full transfer approval |

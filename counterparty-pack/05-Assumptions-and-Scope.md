## 5. Deliverables Expected from Counterparties

### From EPC Bidder:

1. Budget EPC price — PV (lump sum or $/Wp)
2. Budget EPC price — BESS (optional line item)
3. Schedule range to COD (months from NTP)
4. Key exclusions and assumptions
5. Commercial headlines: warranty period, LD stance, payment milestones

### From Offtake Counterparty:

1. Indicative tariff range (¢/kWh or $/MWh)
2. Escalation mechanism
3. Tenor and volume commitment
4. Credit support outline
5. Next steps and timeline to binding term sheet

### From Independent Engineer:

1. Initial gap assessment
2. Interim bankability letter (2–3 week target)
3. Resource validation approach
4. Scope and fee for full technical due diligence
5. Timeline to final bankability opinion
